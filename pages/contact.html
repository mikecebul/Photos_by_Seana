Hi, my name is
Seana Hope

Yes, Seana Hope are both my first names so you can call me whatever is easiest for you. So why photography? I grew up spending my lunch breaks in the library, scanning books and learning about places by looking at the pictures in between the pages. As I got older, I have become so adventurous and with every journey I take, I enjoyed sharing them through my photos. I went to school and graduated with a bachelor's in Journalism, with Photojournalism as my saving grace. It had been such long years. While the spark for writing escaped me, I looked forward to the Film and Photojournalism. They were what I kept holding on to and got me through the years. Like everyone else, I want to capture every moment no matter how little. So if you see me without my camera, no worries because I still have my phone on me that does an awesome job too. I want the world to know that no matter where you're from, the world is at your fingertips. It doesn't matter whether you're actually on a plane going to your next destination, or scanning Instagram looking at the jaw dropping Auroras in Iceland. And through my camera and lenses, I too, will be there to share the world and make moments last for you and your loved ones.
